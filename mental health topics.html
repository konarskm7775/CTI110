Overcoming Depression

Depression is a mental health condition characterized by persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and disinterest in activities once enjoyed. Overcoming depression often involves a combination of therapy, medication, and self-care practices. Simple steps such as maintaining a healthy lifestyle, staying connected with supportive individuals, setting achievable goals, and seeking professional help can significantly aid in managing and overcoming depression.
